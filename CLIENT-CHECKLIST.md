# Client Onboarding Checklist

Use this checklist to gather all necessary details from your Pune-based clients before you start their mini-website.

## 1. Basic Business Info
- [ ] **Business Name:** (e.g., "Glow by Gauri")
- [ ] **Niche:** (e.g., "Bridal Makeup Artist")
- [ ] **Tagline:** A catchy 1-line description of what they do.
- [ ] **WhatsApp Number:** The number where they want to receive bookings.
- [ ] **Instagram Handle:** Link to their profile.
- [ ] **Area/Location:** Specific area in Pune (e.g., "Kothrud", "Baner").
- [ ] **Full Address:** For the Google Maps section.

## 2. Visual Content
- [ ] **Profile Photo/Logo:** High-quality PNG/JPG.
- [ ] **Gallery Images:** 6–9 best photos of their work.
- [ ] **Service Images:** 1 photo per package/service offered.

## 3. Services & Pricing
- [ ] **Service Names:** (e.g., "Full Bridal Package", "Engagement Makeup").
- [ ] **Price:** (e.g., "Starts at ₹15,000" or "₹2,500").
- [ ] **Description:** 1–2 sentences explaining what is included.

## 4. Social Proof & Trust
- [ ] **Testimonials:** 2–4 reviews from happy clients.
- [ ] **Years of Experience:** To build authority.
- [ ] **Happy Clients Count:** Approximate number.

## 5. Logistics
- [ ] **FAQs:** 3–5 common questions they get asked (e.g., "Do you travel?", "Refund policy?").
- [ ] **Process:** 3–4 steps on how a client can book them.

---

## Post-Delivery Steps
1. **Send the Link:** Share the live URL.
2. **Add to Bio:** Help the client add the link to their Instagram Bio.
3. **QR Code:** Generate a QR code for the website and send it to them for their visiting cards or studio.
4. **Tutorial:** Send a 1-minute video showing them how to view the site on mobile.

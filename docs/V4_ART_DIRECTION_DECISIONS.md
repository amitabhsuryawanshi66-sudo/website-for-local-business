# V4 Art Direction Decisions

## Visual Direction: Cinematic Tattoo Concierge

For the V4 Master Template, we moved away from the soft, light-themed boutique aesthetic of V1/V2 to create a high-contrast, premium dark experience. This proves the skill-stack's ability to handle diverse art directions.

### 1. Hero Section (Option A: Cinematic Dark Editorial)
- **Choice:** Selected a split-screen asymmetric composition.
- **Why:** In a tattoo studio context, visual tension and high-impact imagery are the primary trust signals. A standard centered hero would feel "too corporate."
- **Installed Skill:** Used *Taste Skill* rules for asymmetric layouts to break the SaaS-landing-page pattern.

### 2. Gallery (Option A: Editorial Collage)
- **Choice:** A masonry-style bento grid with unequal row/column spans.
- **Why:** Tattoo portfolios are diverse in size and detail. A standard equal grid diminishes the "Art Journal" feeling.
- **Installed Skill:** Applied *21st.dev* variation workflow to prioritize storytelling over information density.

### 3. Service Tiers (Option B: Bold Underground Brand)
- **Choice:** High-contrast cards with minimal glass-morphism backgrounds.
- **Why:** Tattoo pricing is often complex. We used *UI UX Pro Max* information architecture to simplify "Starting prices" and "Time estimates" into a clear, scannable comparison.

### 4. Experience Flow (Option C: Boutique Storytelling)
- **Choice:** A sticky-scroll method where the title stays put as the process steps slide in from the right.
- **Why:** This creates a sense of "Concierge Guidance" rather than a generic "About Us" section.
- **Installed Skill:** Used *Emil Kowalski* motion rules for sequencing and smooth Y-axis slide transitions.

### 5. Sticky Inquiry Bar (Option B: Minimal Luxury)
- **Choice:** A floating capsule (glass-morphism) with high-contrast text.
- **Why:** On Android (360px+), a large bottom-fixed bar can be intrusive. This capsule design is thumb-friendly but remains lightweight.
- **Installed Skill:** *Android-First QA* enforced a 56px height target for the "Start Consultation" button.

### 6. Overall Theme (Cinematic Dark)
- **Primary:** #0A0A0A (Ink)
- **Accent:** #E53E3E (Muted Blood Red)
- **Typography:** Uppercase, tight-tracking headings (Inter Tight).

This V4 build confirms the clean-room process works and the skill-stack is now properly "vendored" into the repository's DNA.

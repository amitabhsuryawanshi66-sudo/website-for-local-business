# 📸 Instagram Bio Optimization

The link in your bio is the only bridge between a follower and a paying customer. Here is how to set it up for maximum clicks.

## 1. Setting the Link
1. Go to your Profile > **Edit Profile**.
2. Tap **Links** > **Add external link**.
3. Paste your new website URL.
4. Give it a title: `Bookings & Packages`.

## 2. The "Call to Action" in Bio
Don't just leave the link. Use the line right above the link to tell people why to click it.

**Good Examples:**
- 👇 **Check Wedding Availability**
- 👇 **See Pricing & Menu**
- 👇 **Book your Room here**
- 👇 **View our Latest Work**

## 3. Use in Stories
- Post a beautiful photo/video of your work.
- Add the **Link Sticker**.
- Customize the sticker text to: `See Packages ✨` or `Check Availability 📅`.

## 4. Professionalism
Having a clean, dedicated website makes you look like a "Brand" rather than just a "Page." You can now charge premium rates because your presentation is premium.
